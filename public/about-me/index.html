Who Am I?

I am a software engineer, people manager and technology visionary. Here is a brief bio about me. I am currently at Quid as Director of Engineering for the web team. Quid web transforms million of data points to beautiful visualizations of information and data powered by big-data analytics.

Previously, I worked at Opower leading the Outbound & Engineers Efficiency team. Opower IPO’ed in 2014 and acquired by Oracle in 2016. For a short time, I left Opower to start my own company, Tilofy, with friends. Tilofy is a real-time search engine which indexes millions of social media content to power nearby insights.

You can find me active on Github working on phantomjs-node or my Stackoverflow account which I am often active.
